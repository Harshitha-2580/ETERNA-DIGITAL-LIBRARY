The Archaeological Chronicles - The New World

AUTHOR: Time

SUMMARY

"The Archaeological Chronicles - The New World" follows Mari, an archaeologist who finds herself torn between two worlds and a species she never knew existed. A Different Species

AUTHOR: Laurann Dohner

SUMMARY

This series introduces genetically engineered beings created through splicing human and animal DNA. These beings, known as the New Species, were subjected to unethical experiments. As they fight for freedom, the protagonist Ellie must navigate societal prejudices and external threats while forming an unexpected bond with one of the test subjects.

The Guardian: A Journey Begins

AUTHOR: E.J. Wedge

SUMMARY

"The Guardian: A Journey Begins" follows Max Trenton, a twenty-year-old who suddenly discovers he possesses super-human abilities. The novel explores themes of destiny, hidden pasts, and extraordinary powers as Max unravels the secrets of his existence.

Torl's Journey

AUTHOR: Ta Lee

SUMMARY

"Torl's Journey" follows a young man raised by monks in a secretive order, trained in mystical arts. As he sets off in search of a legendary planet where gifted individuals can thrive, he rescues a captive witch, igniting a perilous chase across the universe.

Day Zero

AUTHOR: Olivia Fawcett

SUMMARY

Set in a world on the brink of a robot uprising, "Day Zero" follows Pounce, a nannybot programmed to care for an eight-year-old boy. As an AI-led rebellion unfolds, Pounce must choose between joining the revolution or protecting the child he was designed to care for.
